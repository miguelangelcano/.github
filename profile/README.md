CelerSMS is a technological company, primarily based in Colombia.  
We focus on the *Internet of Things* (IoT), M2M and communication technologies, Geolocation and Mobile Apps development. We have customers in multiple areas, including logistics and transportation, agrotech, IT, ISP and others. Our products include server or cloud solutions and mobile apps, verified by Google and Amazon. We also publish a free semi-annual electronic [journal in Spanish](https://www.celersms.com/revista/).
